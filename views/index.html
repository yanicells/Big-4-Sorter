DLSU

De La Salle University

Founded by the Brothers of the Christian Schools in 1911, DLSU is a leading institution in engineering, business, and technology. Known for its commitment to academic innovation and research, the university is recognized for its strong focus on community engagement and service.

ADMU

Ateneo de Manila University

Established by the Society of Jesus in 1859, Ateneo is highly regarded for its liberal arts education, philosophy, and ethical leadership development. The university is noted for its rigorous standards, strong culture of critical thinking, and Jesuit value system emphasizing social justice.

UP

University of the Philippines

As the national university, UP is the country's premier institution for public service, research, and academic freedom. It is known for its diverse student body, highly competitive programs across all fields, and significant role in national development and social advocacy.

UST

University of Santo Tomas

Established in 1611, UST is one of the world's largest Catholic universities and Asia's oldest existing university. It is deeply rooted in Dominican tradition, noted for its excellent programs in medicine, architecture, and the arts, and its rich cultural heritage.
